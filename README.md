**Deskly**, a CRM I’m building to make life easier for freelancers and micro/small businesses. My goal is to create a simple, free, and intuitive platform that helps manage clients, projects, invoices, and more—all in one place. I hope i can create a tool that solves real problems for independent professionals, artists and small teams.

## The Idea Behind Deskly

As a small business owner and freelancer myself, I’ve experienced firsthand how challenging it can be to stay organized while managing multiple clients, projects, and deadlines. That’s why I started Deskly: to build a tool that simplifies the day-to-day tasks of running a freelance gig or a small business. I want Deskly to be the go-to solution for freelancers and micro/small businesses who need to:

- Keep track of client details, interactions, and project statuses.
- Set deadlines and monitor progress across multiple projects.
- Create invoices, send estimates, and manage payments.
- Organize tasks with to-do lists for each project.
- Share files with clients directly in the app.
- Get a quick overview of income, deadlines, and tasks through a custom dashboard.

My vision is to make Deskly a free, user-friendly CRM that freelancers and small business owners can rely on, without the complexity or cost of other tools on the market.
